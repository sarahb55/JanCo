About Us

Last fall, grassroots activist organizations sharing a strong interest in social justice and women’s rights began working on an event to advance the values of the Women’s March Movement of fairness, inclusion, and dignity for all people, especially those most marginalized by a divisive and harmful agenda. The newly formed team presenting this event has named itself the January Coalition. Together, we agreed upon a Statement of Values, and we are dedicated to producing an event that will move forward the same kinds of progressive causes that were supported by the 2017 march.

This coalition is new, but our member organizations and individuals have been pursuing social justice in Massachusetts for many decades. Our current list of participating organizations is below, but we are very interested in including anyone who shares our core values, particularly groups advocating for marginalized communities.
