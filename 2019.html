Voices in Action: A Celebration of Women

Details

The January Coalition had originally envisioned an event on January 12th that embraced all communities of women. We chose this date to avoid co-opting the important celebrations for Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. weekend. However, it has become clear that having two large-scale womenâ€™s events a week apart is not constructive for our community, and our team wished to avoid further divisiveness and dilution of impact. The January Coalition has decided to re-envision our event on January 12th and postpone it to a later date. We remain an inclusive grassroots team opposed to all forms of discrimination (including anti-Semitism, Islamophobia, transphobia, ableism, racism, and all other oppressions) and committed to putting our ideals into political and social action.

Accessibility

Accessibility to people with disabilities, visible and invisible, is a priority for this event. Closer to the event we will have information on the Accessible Seating Area, CART Captioning, Restrooms, Sensory Area, Service Animal Relief Area, and opportunities to volunteer.

How can we support this event?

Share the word with your friends. Show up. Bring a crowd with you. Financially, we depend on individual donations to make this event happen. Your contribution helps cover the costs of a stage, sound equipment, portable restrooms, a generator, etc., Massachusetts Peace Action, a 501c3 non-profit, is handling donations for the rally.
